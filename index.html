What we expect?

Our basic requirements are below

Time

Our Staff will work for 8 hrs on all working days, and Overtime will be charged for working after hours or on Holidays.

Conveyance

Conveyance need not be provided for staff and they depend on their own means.

Wages

The wages will be revised yearly once as per the Minimum Wage Act announced by Government of Karnataka i.e., in the month of April every year, w.e.f 01 st of April.

Payment

Payment need to be made within 5 th every month against our bill.

Contract

The contract can be terminated by giving Two month’s prior notice from both side of the party.
